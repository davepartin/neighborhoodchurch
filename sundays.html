What Happens on Sunday Morning

Sundays at Neighborhood Church are designed to be welcoming, authentic, and centered on Jesus. Our service starts at 10:30 AM for worship, teaching, prayer, and communion.

When you arrive, you'll find coffee and friendly faces ready to greet you. We sing together, pray together, hear God's Word taught through books of the Bible, and take communion regularly to remember what Jesus has done for us.

We Care about Our Neighborhood Kids

We believe children are a vital part of our church family. During the sermon, kids head to age-appropriate classes where they learn about Jesus through engaging lessons, activities, and caring teachers. Parents, you can worship knowing your children are safe, loved, and learning about the God who loves them.

What Should I Wear?

Come as you are. Seriously. You'll see everything from jeans and t-shirts to button-ups and dresses. We care about your heart, not your wardrobe.

Our focus is on authenticity, not appearance. Whether you're coming straight from the gym or dressed for a fancy brunch after church, you'll fit right in. What matters most is that you're here.

Common Questions

Where do I park?

We have plenty of parking in our lot at 8600 W 91st Terrace. When you arrive, just look for open spots. There's no reserved parking. If you have mobility concerns, we have accessible spots near the entrance.

What's the service like?

Our service is casual and warm. We sing together (contemporary worship songs), pray, hear a sermon from the Bible, and occasionally share communion. The teaching is conversational and practical. Expect seminary-level depth delivered with everyday clarity.

Will I have to stand out or introduce myself?

Absolutely not. We're glad you're here, but we won't put you on the spot. You're welcome to connect at your own pace. If you want to meet people, we'll introduce you. If you'd rather observe quietly, that's completely fine too.

Is there childcare?

Yes! We offer children's classes from birth to age 5 throughout the service, and age-appropriate classes for K-5th grade during the sermon portion. Your children will be cared for by background-checked volunteers in a safe, engaging environment where they'll learn about Jesus.

What if I'm not a Christian or I'm exploring faith?

You're especially welcome. We believe honest questions are a sign of genuine faith, and we're honored to be part of your journey. You won't be judged, pressured, or made to feel like an outsider. Come with your doubts, your questions, and your curiosity. We're here to walk alongside you.

Can I watch online?

Yes! We livestream our services on YouTube every Sunday at 10:30 AM. While we'd love to meet you in person, we understand life happens. You can find our channel at youtube.com/@neighborhoodchurchop.

Ready to Visit?

We can't wait to meet you. Come this Sunday and see what it's like to be part of a church family that's learning to love God and love our neighbors together.
